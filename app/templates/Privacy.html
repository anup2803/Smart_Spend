Privacy Policy - Smart Spend

Privacy Policy

Information We Collect

  • User registration details (name, email, password).
  • Financial records entered by the user (income, expenses, goals).

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide personalized financial tracking and reporting.
  • To generate charts, summaries, and savings insights.

Data Protection

  • Passwords are stored in encrypted format.
  • Data is protected using standard database security measures.
  • No personal financial data will be shared with third parties.

User Rights

  • Users can add, edit, or delete their financial records anytime.
  • Users may request permanent deletion of their account and data.

Data Retention

User data is stored as long as the account is active.

Deleted accounts will have their associated data removed.
